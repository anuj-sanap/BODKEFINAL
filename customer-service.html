Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How can I track my order?

After your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website or the courier's website.

Which payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, net banking, UPI, and cash on delivery for orders within India.

Is international shipping available?

Yes, we offer shipping to most countries worldwide. International shipping usually takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

What is the process for returning a saree?

You can initiate a return within 7 days of receiving your order. Please refer to our Returns & Exchanges section for more details.

Shipping & Delivery

Domestic Shipping

We offer free shipping on all orders above ₹5,000 within India. For orders below ₹5,000, a shipping fee of ₹150 will be applied.

Standard delivery within India takes 3-5 business days.

Express delivery (1-2 business days) is available at an additional cost of ₹250.

International Shipping

International shipping rates vary depending on the destination country and the weight of the package.

Standard international shipping takes 7-14 business days.

Express international shipping (3-5 business days) is available for select countries.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email.

You can track your order by entering the tracking number on our website or the courier's website.

Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery if the saree is unused, unwashed, and in its original packaging with all tags intact.

Please note that customized or made-to-order sarees cannot be returned or exchanged.

Exchange Process

If you wish to exchange your saree for a different color or design, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your order.

Exchange is subject to the availability of the desired saree.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect the returned saree, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days.

The refund will be credited to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Payment Options

Online Payment

We accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and RuPay.

You can also pay using net banking, UPI, or digital wallets like Google Pay, PhonePe, and Paytm.

Cash on Delivery

Cash on Delivery is available for orders within India up to ₹15,000.

A nominal COD fee of ₹50 will be applied to all Cash on Delivery orders.

EMI Options

EMI options are available for orders above ₹3,000 with select banks.

The EMI tenure ranges from 3 to 12 months depending on the bank and the order value.

Saree Care

Silk Sarees

Dry clean only for silk sarees to maintain their luster and texture.

Store in a cool, dry place wrapped in a cotton cloth to prevent damage.

Avoid direct sunlight as it may fade the colors.

Cotton Sarees

Hand wash with mild detergent in cold water.

Do not wring or twist the saree. Instead, gently squeeze out excess water.

Air dry in shade to prevent color fading.

General Care Tips

Iron on low to medium heat depending on the fabric.

Store sarees by folding them properly to avoid creases.

Rotate your sarees regularly to prevent pressure marks.
